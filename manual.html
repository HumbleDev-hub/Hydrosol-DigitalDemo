Operational Manual

Advanced Digital Twin Sterilization

The Hydrosol HS-2000 is a state-of-the-art water purification and sterilization system utilizing multi-stage filtration and UV-C light technology. This manual provides the necessary steps for hardware integration and long-term upkeep.

Note: This model is equipped with a Solar-Active grid. Ensure the top panel has access to ambient or direct light for optimal battery health.

Initial Setup

1 Unboxing: Ensure all seals on the smoky glass reservoir are intact.

2 Placement: Place the unit on a flat surface with at least 5cm of clearance for the bottom intake valves.

3 Priming: Fill the reservoir halfway and run the "Flush" cycle via the front interface.

Device Operation

The Interface

The front black panel displays real-time flow rate and sterilization status. A pulsing blue light from the internal pods indicates the UV-C system is actively neutralizing pathogens.

Safety Warning: Do not attempt to open the top lid while the UV-C pods are active. The system includes a safety cutoff, but manual override is discouraged.

Maintenance Guide

To ensure the HS-2000 operates at peak efficiency, follow the maintenance schedule below.

Carbon Filter Replacement

The Active Carbon Pod (located on the far left) should be replaced every 6 months or 2,000 liters.

Cleaning the Reservoir

1. Drain all water via the bottom release.
2. Use a soft microfiber cloth to wipe the internal glass.
3. Avoid abrasive chemicals that may degrade the smoky finish.

Intake Valve Cleaning

Inspect the bottom intake circles monthly. Clear any debris that may obstruct the flow rate.
